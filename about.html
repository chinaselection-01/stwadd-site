Who We Are

Yongkang STWADD Houseware Co., Ltd. was established in 2017, is a research and development, production, sales, service in one of the integrated enterprises, supplying stainless steel bottles, tumblers, explosion-proof thermos bottles, plastic bottles, plastic tumblers, water bottles and other stable quality products made of metal and plastic.

Equipped with an advanced automated production line with a quarter turnover of 1 million pieces. Our design team provides ODM and OEM services. Our products strictly follow the EU and America food grade standards, best-selling in European and American markets.
