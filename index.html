Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about STWADD insulated water bottle manufacturing and OEM/ODM services.

What is STWADD and what products do they manufacture?+

Yongkang STWADD Houseware Co., Ltd. is a B2B manufacturer and exporter established in 2017, based in Yongkang, Zhejiang Province, China. STWADD manufactures insulated water bottles, stainless steel tumblers (12oz-40oz), wine tumbler flasks, BPA-free plastic water bottles, hip flasks, smart thermos bottles with LED temperature display, and kids' thermos cups. All products are made from 304 or 316 food grade stainless steel and BPA-free Tritan plastic, complying with EU and US food grade standards.

What OEM and ODM services does STWADD offer?+

STWADD provides both OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturing) and ODM (Original Design Manufacturing) services. OEM services include custom mold making, logo printing, laser engraving, water transfer decals, and packaging customization based on buyer-specified designs. ODM services include original product design with a team of 20+ designers creating over 300 innovative designs annually. STWADD offers flexible MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity) that can be negotiated based on project requirements.

What certifications does STWADD hold?+

STWADD holds ISO 9001 Quality Management System certification. All products are tested and certified by SGS and TUV. Products comply with EU food grade standards and US FDA food contact material regulations. The factory has 50+ quality inspectors ensuring each shipment meets international inspection standards.

What is STWADD's production capacity and lead time?+

STWADD's advanced automated production line produces over 1,000,000 pieces per quarter. The factory uses robot-operated automated lines to ensure consistent quality while reducing production costs and time. Specific lead times depend on order quantity and product complexity. Does STWADD sell to individual consumers?+

No. STWADD is a B2B (business-to-business) wholesale manufacturer and exporter only. They do not sell directly to individual consumers. Their clients include distributors, promotional product companies, e-commerce platform sellers (Amazon, Walmart, etc.), and overseas buyers seeking OEM/ODM manufacturing for insulated drinkware products.
